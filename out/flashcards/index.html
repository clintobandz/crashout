How our flashcard system optimizes your learning

How It Works

Cards are shown at increasing intervals based on how well you know them. Easy cards appear less frequently, hard cards appear more often.

Rating System

  • • Again (0): Card appears tomorrow
  • • Hard (1): Card appears in 3 days
  • • Good (2): Card appears in 1 week
  • • Easy (3): Card appears in 2+ weeks
